Howdy!

My name is Griffin, I'm what you would call a "professional people-person." I love movies, the outdoors, music, and creative expression. As a professional as well as a hobbyist, I always strive for new ways to stretch the limits of art, all while gaining more experience and enjoying myself. In that sense, I see photography as much less than a job and more-so a passion. Feel free to check out my Instagram page for updates on my projects! If any stand out to you, let me know! I'm happy to make custom prints for anyone.

For over 6 years, I have been passionately taking photos and sharing art with my friends and family. I recently decided to break into the professional photography and printing industry, as I believe I bring something to the table that I don't see in many other photographers.
I wouldn't offer a service or sell a product that I, myself, wouldn't buy. I also don't believe in charging extra or limiting the amount of pictures during the editing process, these are your pictures, why shouldn't you have them?

One of the aspects of family and portrait photography that I see is missed is the human element - staged photos are obviously great if that's a style you're going for, but in my experience, my clients and friends prefer the candid shots I take. I believe that a photography session should be relaxed, conversational, and (most importantly) fun! If it's not fun, why bother?

If you're on the lookout for a portrait, family, or event photographer, I hope you give me a chance to show you what it's like to work with me.
